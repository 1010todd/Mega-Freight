Adds several mostly civilian ships for you and npc merchants( and pirates) to use for more variety in human space.

This plugin add over 45 freighters and other civilian ships as well as a few warships used by many factions (and some aliens), special jobs and bigger jobs to fill them and more.

Requires Latest Unstable Build/0.10.5+

For turret arc limit requires latest Continuous build (since 2024/05/11)
